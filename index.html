what is

city x nft?

The fate of works by the most remarkable Ukrainian avant-garde artists is currently unknown because of the ongoing war in Ukraine.

The City X project aims to preserve the Municipal Gallery and Vovatanya Gallery’s collections forever.

The NFT project is inspired by the real art collection from the two galleries, the fate of which is currently unknown because of the ongoing war in Ukraine.

2009, oil print, 90x56 cm.

The funny, ironic, sarcastic series "About Hares"

According to the author's idea, the main hero is the twin brother of a human individual at a certain level of his material well-being. He has reached overseas countries as either a tourist or a newly arrived emigrant and poses at the main symbol of democratic America.
Which brought the author wide popularity in Ukraine and the love of the audience, did not only stop on socio-political realities, «topics of the day» and everyday troubles in her native Fatherland.
